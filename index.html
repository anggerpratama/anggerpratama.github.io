About Me

Hello! I'm Angger Pratamadhita, a passionate Fullstack Web Developer based in Indonesia. I thrive on building robust and dynamic web applications from concept to deployment, ensuring seamless user experiences and efficient backend operations.

My core expertise lies in a versatile tech stack, including Nuxt.js for crafting intuitive front-end interfaces, and a strong command over Node.js and Express.js for powerful backend development. I'm proficient in TypeScript, ensuring scalable and maintainable codebases. For databases, I'm well-versed in PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MongoDB, and I have extensive experience in designing and implementing REST APIs. My skills also extend to traditional web development with PHP and Laravel.

Beyond my primary stack, I've also gained valuable experience with C# .NET Core and MS SQL Server, further broadening my capabilities in enterprise-level application development. I am a firm believer in continuous learning and eagerly embrace new technologies and frameworks to stay at the forefront of the ever-evolving web development landscape.

When I'm not immersed in code, you'll often find me unwinding through gaming, which helps me maintain a fresh perspective and fuels my creativity. I'm always excited to tackle new challenges and collaborate on innovative projects!
